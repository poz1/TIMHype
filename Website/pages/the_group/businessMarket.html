Business & Market

Telecom Italia S.p.A. is engaged in fixed and mobile telecommunications services in Italy and the holding company for various businesses, principally telecommunications. The Company operates through four business units: Domestic, Brazil, Media and Olivetti. The main categories of accesses are fixed telephony accesses, Internet and data accesses, Internet Protocol TV (IPTV), mobile accesses (includes mobile telephony), unbundled local loop, wholesale asymmetric digital subscriber line (ADSL) and others. Fixed telephony accesses include public switched telephone network (PSTN) lines, integrated services digital network (ISDN) lines and circuits. Internet and data accesses include broadband accesses (wholesale ADSL and retail ADSL lines), narrowband accesses (Internet service through the PSTN) and other accesses (unbundled local loops, circuits and other business data accesses including WiFi and fiber optic cable).
